Made from Liam James Kay video - 9 New Ai tools that will BLOW YOUR MIND (Must See) Also used other videos and references, with my research too.

Like ChatGPT

  • ChatGPT - Nothing to say about it, baby God
  • Perplexity - Like ChatGPT but with lot of modification, limited results.
  • New Bing Chat - ChatGPT at backend, integrated into search

Other

  • runwayml - Suite of tools, generator of images, in painting, removing items from video clips.
  • Rewindai - Record everything you do on MAC (only mac, not pc) and analyse it and search on timeline. Like a video you watch, even content said in that video can be found.
    • Heyday - Similar to rewindAI, but on browser.
  • tome.app - Make presentation using AI.
  • bhuman.ai - Like video mail merge. Choose a ai person template, connect to spreadsheet and then customised videos are created.
  • browse AI - Extract/ scrap any data from any website, then do any thing in automated way. Build pipeline.
    • socialtradia - Buy sell social insta accounts, nothing to do with AI.

Video and Art

  • d-id.com - AI generated people who can speak. Your image or dall-e generated presenter. Text to any presenter speaking.
  • MidJourney - A discord group to generate images and art. Then combine those arts and sell on ETSY Printful.
    • Watch The Secret to making $250 per Day with AI Art by Liam James Kay
    • Use erank or marmalaid to research what sells on etsy and then make art
  • BlueWillow - Like MidJourney, but free
  • Lexica - Like MidJourney or BlueWillow
  • Pictory.ai - Video Generation and editing related

Sound, Voice and Music

  • SounDraw.io - Music Creation
  • ElevenLabs - Nice Voices from text
  • ResembleAI - Text to Voice
  • Voice.AI - Real Time Voice Change
  • Play.ht - High Quality Audio Voices from text
  • descript - Realistic copy of your voice and text to speech, speech to text.

Music and Singing

  • MusicLM - By Google, but not available as such. Text to Music
  • Dreamtonics - Synthesizer V Audio editor, text to singing- https://dreamtonics.com/synthesizerv-gen1/en/download/
  • VoiceMod - Text to Singing, limited to few templates. - https://tuna.voicemod.net/text-to-song/ and https://www.voicemod.net/
